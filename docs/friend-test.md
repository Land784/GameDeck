# Trying GameDeck (thanks!)

You are helping test **GameDeck**, a little Windows app that lets you change
your music and skip YouTube ads with keyboard shortcuts and a small overlay,
without alt-tabbing out of your game.

This takes about ten minutes. There are no wrong answers. If something
confuses you or does not work, that is exactly what I want to hear about.

## 1. Install it

1. Download `GameDeck-x.y.z-win-x64.zip` from the
   [Releases page](https://github.com/Land784/GameDeck/releases).
2. Right-click the zip, choose "Extract All", and pick any folder.
3. Open the folder and run `GameDeck.exe`.
4. **If Windows shows a blue "Windows protected your PC" box:** that is
   normal for a new app that is not from the store. Click **More info**,
   then **Run anyway**. (If this made you nervous, tell me, that is useful.)
5. No window opens. GameDeck lives in the system tray, near the clock. Look
   for its icon there. That means it is running.

There is no installer and nothing to clean up later. To remove it, close it
from the tray and delete the folder.

## 2. Play with it

Start some music first (Spotify, YouTube in a browser, Apple Music, anything).
Then try these, in and out of a game:

- Press **Ctrl+Alt+Space** to play/pause. Does your music respond?
- Press **Ctrl+Alt+Right** and **Ctrl+Alt+Left** to change track.
- Press **Ctrl+Alt+O**. A small card showing the current song should fade in
  and then fade out. Does it show up?
- Now launch a game and do all of the above while it is running. Try it in
  **borderless** or **windowed** mode, and also in **fullscreen**.
  - The shortcuts should work no matter what.
  - The card may or may not appear over fullscreen games. If it does not
    appear over a fullscreen game, that is a known limitation, but please
    still tell me which game and which mode.
- Right-click the tray icon and open **Settings**. Poke around. Anything
  confusing?

Optional, if you use YouTube: there is a companion browser extension for
skipping ads from in-game. Setup is in the
[README](https://github.com/Land784/GameDeck/blob/main/README.md#setting-up-ad-skip).
Skip this if it is a hassle.

## 3. Tell me what you found

Two easy ways, whichever you prefer:

- **File it on GitHub** (best): go to the
  [New issue page](https://github.com/Land784/GameDeck/issues/new/choose),
  pick **Friend test feedback**, and fill in the boxes. One issue per thing
  you noticed is perfect.
- **Or just message me** your notes. Screenshots or a short screen recording
  help a lot, but plain text is fine.

Things worth reporting: anything that did not work, anything that was
confusing, anything that felt slow or rough, and anything you liked. Thank
you!
